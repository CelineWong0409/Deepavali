Happy Deepavali!

Welcome to the Deepavali Celebration!

Deepavali, also known as the Festival of Lights, symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Join us in celebrating this joyous occasion with family, friends, and lots of lights!


How We Celebrate

  • Lighting diyas (oil lamps)
  • Decorating with rangoli (colorful patterns)
  • Sharing sweets and gifts
  • Fireworks and festivities
